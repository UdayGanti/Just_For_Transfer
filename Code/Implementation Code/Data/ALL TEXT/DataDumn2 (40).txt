
Overview

The University of South Florida provides employees the opportunity to participate
               in a variety of benefit programs. Voluntary programs include health, life, dental,
               vision, accident / disability, long term care, tax sheltered annuities and many others.
               All employees participate in one of four retirement plans.
Please review the Benefits Summary for a quick overview on the benefits you can participate in as an employee with USF.
In addition to the essential benefits, USF recognizes the need for a healthy work/life
               balance and offers many opportunities for professional development and personal enrichment
               through our Employee Tuition Program, Employee Assistance Program workshops, Discounts
               and Perks Program and On-Campus Child care.
