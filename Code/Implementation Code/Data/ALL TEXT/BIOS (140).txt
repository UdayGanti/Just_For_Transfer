Varol O. KayhanAssociate Professorvkayhan@usf.eduCampus: St. PetersburgRoom: LPH 428Phone: 727-873-4006VitaVarol Kayhan is an associate professor in the School of Information Systems and Management
               in St. Petersburg. He has been with USF since 2010 and teaches students the techniques
               of understanding data visualization and machine learning and how that can be relevant
               to careers and solve problems in everyday life.Kayhan holds a Microsoft Professional Program Certification for Artificial Intelligence
               and is an AWS Certified Solutions Architect at the associate level. Two of his courses
               have been certified by Quality Matters and his research has been published in numerous
               refereed journals, includingBig Data,Behavior Research MethodsandInformation & Management.
