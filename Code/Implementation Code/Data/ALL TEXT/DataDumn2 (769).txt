
Overview
The USF Muma College of Business offers a some non-credit certificates for those wanting
               to expand their knowledge without the need to enroll in a full degree program. The
               programs have been created to focus on areas of interest to the current business climate. 
These non-credit short-duration certificate programs are listed on the left and are
               offered in a mix of one-week, multi-week, in-person or online options.
USF Continuing and Professional Education also offers a myriad of certificate programs for life-long learners.  Topics range
               from basic economic development to human resources skills to business process improvement and Lean Six Sigma certification.  View the full list of USF CTPE programs.
