
National Weather Service
As part of the University's StormReady University designation, USF offers the National
               Weather Service's SKYWARN Spotter Trainings to the USF community. These courses provide
               valuable knowledge on severe weather attributes and reporting information. Participants
               who successfully complete the courses may seek certification as National Weather Service
               Storm Spotters. The following link provides both in-person and online training opportunities
               available to the west central and southwest Florida areas.

NWS Tampa Bay SKYWARN® Program

Please send certificates to USF-EmergencyMgmt@usf.edu in order to receive credit in GEMS.
