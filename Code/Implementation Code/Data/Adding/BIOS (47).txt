William “Mike” SinclairAssociate Instructorwsinclair@usf.eduCampus: St. PetersburgRoom: LPH 319GVitaMike Sinclair is an associate instructor in the Lynn Pippenger School of Accountancy
               in St. Petersburg. With vast experience in the public and private corporate environments,
               he teaches courses related to auditing, federal income taxation, federal taxation
               of business entities and principles of financial accounting. He has taught at USF
               since 2013 and was awarded Delta Sigma Pi’s Order of the Silver Helmet for 25 years
               of service to the organization.He has worked at large consumer product companies, including Unilever and Energizer
               Holdings, and helped co-found a sales and marketing consulting firm. Sinclair has
               also worked at the IRS since 2007, where he currently serves as a revenue agent. He
               is a licenced CPA and also a member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners
               and the National Association of Certified Valuation Analysts.Sinclair earned a master's degree in accounting from Georgia Southern University and
               his bachelor's degree from the University of South Florida.TeachingACG 2021 - Principles of Financial AccountingTAX 4001 - Concepts of Federal Income TaxationServiceMuma Faculty Executive Committee (Fall 2020 – present)LPSOA Undergraduate Curriculum Committee (Spring 2020 – present)Faculty Advisor, Delta Sigma Pi (Fall 2014 – present)
