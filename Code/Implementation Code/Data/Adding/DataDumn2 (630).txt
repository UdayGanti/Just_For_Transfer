
Overview
The main goal of Emergency Management at the University of South Florida is to enhance
               the safety of the students, faculty, staff, and visitors. We strive to provide awareness
               to our community in an effort to be as prepared as possible before, during, and after
               an event.
Click on each hazard on the left to learn more about potential hazards and ways that
               you can prepare.
You can also complete the online Emergency Protective Actions training to learn more
               about hazards and ways that you can prepare and protect yourself.
