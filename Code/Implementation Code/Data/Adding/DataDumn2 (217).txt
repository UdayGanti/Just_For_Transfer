
Events
Each semester the Bellini Center and USF host student focused events in the areas
               of professional and career development. Additional events may be added throughout
               the semester. Please click the links for more information and to register. Students in Level 3 and 4 of the Bellini Certification Program must attend one career event listed to fufill level requirements. Attendance at any of the events below meet that
               requirement. Please note events may be restricted to specific majors and the campus
               location. Question regarding an event or level requirement? Contact bellinicenter@usf.edu.
