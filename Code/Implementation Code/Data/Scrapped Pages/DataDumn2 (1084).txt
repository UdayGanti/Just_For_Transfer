CURrent Fact book
The Fact Book, also known as Pocket Facts, is a USF annual publication that provides
               a statistical overview of USF.  It allows prospective students, faculty and administration
               a brief look at our overall accomplishments as well as  statistical information on
               diversity, finance and research, faculty and instructional quality, enrollment, and
               more!
