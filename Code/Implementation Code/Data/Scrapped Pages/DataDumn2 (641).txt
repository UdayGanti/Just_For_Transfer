
Overview
On this website you will find helpful links to University of South Florida regulations
               and University policies, information regarding new and proposed regulations and policies,
               as well as a searchable archive of regulations and policies that have been adopted
               or repealed. 
For information on Delegations of General Authority and Signatory please visit Office of General Counsel - Governance.
If you have any questions or concerns, or would like to subscribe to the regulations
               and policies listserv, please email RegulationsPolicies@usf.edu.
