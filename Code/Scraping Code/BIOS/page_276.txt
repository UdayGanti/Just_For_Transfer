               Business and Finance in St. Petersburg, where she helps students expand their knowledge
               in courses such as international finance and financial modeling, focusing on financial
               theories and concepts.She joined USF in 2019, coming from Villanova University where she served as faculty

               and is the recipient of the Financial Services Exchange Research Award, the Oxford-Yale
               Research Grant and the National Natural Science Foundation of China Research Grant.She earned a PhD from the University of Georgia, and MBA and master's degree from
