                  				Recreation & Wellness
                  			
Recreation & Wellness on our Tampa campus keeps students moving with programs and
                  facilities including a 28,000 square-foot, fully-wired fitness center, group fitness
                  classes, aquatics, intramural sports, club sports, outdoor recreation opportunities,
                  and adventure trips. Our branch campuses also offer robust recreation services.
