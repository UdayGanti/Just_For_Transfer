
Skypad Gaming Room


 

 

The Skypad Gaming Room is located on the 4th floor behind the Skypad Lounge. The room
               consist of private video gaming stations, comfortable chairs, and individual sound
               systems.
In addition, this space includes a large lounge area featuring study tables, white-boards, vending machines, and a microwave. Large
               high-definition televisions are provided for both educational and recreational purposes.
