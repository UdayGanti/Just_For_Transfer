
Comprehensive Emergency Management Plan
Comprehensive emergency management can be defined as the preparation for and the carrying
               out of all emergency functions necessary to mitigate, prepare for, respond to, and
               recover from emergencies and disasters caused by all hazards, whether natural, technological,
               or human caused. The Department maintains a Comprehensive Emergency Management Plan
               (CEMP) that describes the actions taken in the event of an emergency or disaster that
               impacting USF.
