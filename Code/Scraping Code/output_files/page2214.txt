Working in tandem with USF's Career Services, the Collier Student Success Center at
               the USF Muma College of Business assists students with career planning, operates living-learning
               communities and leads study abroad programs.  Special programs such as the Business
               Honors Program, BizComm Center and Corporate Mentor Program are also part of the center.  Undergraduate
               advising services for the business school also falls underneath the Collier Student
               Success Center umbrella, too, as do the services provided by the Office of Employer
               Relations.
