
Overview

The General Accounting department mission is to provide customer service oriented
               support while safeguarding University resources. We provide accurate and audited bank
               reconciliations; monitoring and oversight of fixed asset accounting; consistent and
               reliable transaction processing, timely and accurate state tax reporting and overall
               accounting guidance for the University community.
General Accounting processes the State of Florida's monthly and quarterly sales, tourist
               and communication tax returns. We also research and request unclaimed property that
               may belong to the University or one of its direct support organizations.
Our location is Tampa Campus, Student Services Building, SVC0077
Mail Point: SVC 1039
Our business hours are Monday - Friday 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
